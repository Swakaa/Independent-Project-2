# Independent-Project-2 Name Generator

The Ghanaian name generator!

This is a web application that takes your birthday details and calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender, gives you an Akan Name. Akan names are originally from Ghana.In Ghana,children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

How to find out what your Akan name is, simply fill the form below and click generate name button.
